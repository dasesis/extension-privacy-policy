Privacy Policy

UwU Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from users.

Information Collection and Use

This extension operates entirely on your device and does not send any information to external servers or third parties.

All changes made by the extension (e.g., page titles, placeholders, visual tweaks) are local and temporary within your browser.

Supported Websites

The extension currently modifies the following websites for cosmetic and functional improvements:

  • roblox.com
  • youtube.com (playlist enhancements only)

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties.

Permissions

The extension uses host permissions to apply changes to supported websites. These are used solely for local functionality:

  • *://*.roblox.com/* – for Roblox UI tweaks and features
  • *://www.youtube.com/* – for playlist-related visual changes

Contact

If you have any questions, feel free to contact the developer directly.
