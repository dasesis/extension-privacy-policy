Privacy Policy

UwU Roblox Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from users.

Information Collection and Use

This extension operates entirely on your device and does not send any information to external servers or third parties.

All changes made by the extension (e.g., page titles, placeholders, visual tweaks) are local and temporary within your browser.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties.

Permissions

The extension uses host permissions to apply changes to roblox.com pages. These are used solely for the intended local functionality.

Contact

If you have any questions, feel free to contact the developer directly.
